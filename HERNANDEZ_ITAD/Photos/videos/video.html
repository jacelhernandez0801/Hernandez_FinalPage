Romance

Along for the Ride

Director: Sofia Alvarez

        The movie tells the story of a young woman named Auden who decides to spend the summer before college with her father. While there, she meets Eli, a mysterious and charismatic loner. As Auden navigates her own personal struggles and family dynamics, she finds herself drawn to Eli, leading to a transformative summer of self-discovery, love, and unexpected challenges.

Anyone But You

Director: Will Gluck

         In "Anyone But You," Bea and Ben's promising start quickly fizzles out. However, when they coincidentally attend a destination wedding in Australia, they pretend to be a couple to avoid awkwardness. As they navigate the charade, old feelings resurface, leading to unexpected twists and romantic possibilities. This romantic comedy promises laughter, surprises, and the chance for love against a beautiful backdrop.

Comedy

White Chicks

Director: Keenen Ivory Wayans

        "White Chicks" follows FBI agents Kevin and Marcus Copeland, played by Shawn and Marlon Wayans, who disguise themselves as white women to protect heiresses from a kidnapping plot. Amidst hilarious mishaps and high society antics, they navigate their undercover roles while unraveling secrets. Directed by Keenen Ivory Wayans, this comedy offers uproarious laughs and a quirky take on identity and friendship.

National Security

Director: Dennis Dugan

         "National Security" follows Martin Lawrence as Earl Montgomery, a former LAPD officer wrongly accused of a crime, and Steve Zahn as Hank Rafferty, a security guard. They team up to clear Earl's name and uncover a smuggling ring. Directed by Dennis Dugan, the film blends action, comedy, and buddy dynamics as they navigate challenges and forge an unlikely partnership.

Horror

Insidious: The Red Door

Director: Patrick Wilson

"Insidious: The Red Door" is a horror film that delves into the supernatural realm of the "Insidious" franchise. The story centers around a family who moves into a new home, only to discover a mysterious red door in the attic that seems to be a portal to another dimension. As they investigate the origins of the door, they unwittingly unleash malevolent forces that threaten to consume them. With chilling suspense and terrifying encounters, "Insidious: The Red Door" explores themes of family, fear, and the darkness lurking just beyond reality's veil.

Smile

Director: Parker Finn

"Smile" by Parker Finn follows Alex, a teen with crooked teeth struggling with confidence. He decides to get braces, embarking on a journey of self-acceptance and discovering the true meaning of beauty. Through humor and relatable characters, the story resonates with readers of all ages, celebrating imperfections and the courage to smile, offering a heartwarming reminder that true beauty comes from within and shines brightest when embraced with confidence, inspiring readers to embrace their uniqueness and find joy in being themselves.
